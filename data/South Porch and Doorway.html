South Porch and Doorway

The South Porch has been rebuilt and the door is modern: but the doorway is old and can be dated, by the \'dog-tooth\' moulding which adorns it, to the early years of the thirteenth century. It has probably been moved at each successive widening of the nave.

The carved heads of King Edwin of Northumbria and Paulinus, Bishop of York AD627

The two figure heads at the porch entrance depict King Edwin and Bishop Paulinus, the two father-figures who consolidated Christianity in Ilkley and herabouts, and to whom is attributed the founding of this Church in AD 627
